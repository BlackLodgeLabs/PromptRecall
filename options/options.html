what is prompt recall?

prompt recall is a chrome extension designed to help you save and manage your ai prompts directly from various ai tool websites. all your prompts are saved securely in your chrome storage, ensuring your privacy and allowing seamless synchronization across your devices. it provides a seamless experience for prompt engineers, writers, and anyone who frequently interacts with large language models, allowing you to build a personal library of useful prompts.

how to use

  1. visit any supported ai tool site (e.g., chatgpt, gemini).
  2. type your prompt and get a response.
  3. prompt recall automatically saves your prompt.
  4. open the extension popup to view, open, copy or delete your saved prompts.
  5. manage prompts settings on this page, including which sites are captured from.
