what is prompt recall?

Prompt Recall is the essential Chrome extension built for prompt engineers, writers, and anyone who lives in large language models. It provides a seamless experience, allowing you to save and manage your best AI prompts directly from any AI tool website. Forget cloud worries. All your prompts are saved securely in your Chrome storage, ensuring your data remains private and under your control. This also enables seamless synchronization across all your devices, giving you the power to finally build your personal, permanent library of useful prompts..

how to use

  1. visit any supported ai tool site (e.g., chatgpt, gemini).
  2. type your prompt and get a response.
  3. prompt recall automatically saves your prompt.
  4. open the extension popup to view, open, copy or delete your saved prompts.
  5. manage prompts settings on this page, including which sites are captured from.
